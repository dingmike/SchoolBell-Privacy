Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

🔔 SchoolBell is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information. Our philosophy is simple: We do not collect your data.

1. Data Collection and Usage

SchoolBell is a local utility application. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personally identifiable information (PII). All your schedule settings, bell configurations, and preferences are stored exclusively on your device's local storage (UserDefaults) and are never uploaded to any servers.

2. Permissions and Their Purpose

  • Notifications: Used solely to trigger the bell alerts and reminders at your scheduled times.
  • Background Audio: Used to ensure high-fidelity playback of your selected bell sounds even when the screen is locked or the app is in the background.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party SDKs, tracking tools, or advertising networks. Your behavior within the app is not monitored by any third parties.

4. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, the security of your information is protected by iOS system encryption. Deleting the app will also remove all locally stored settings.
